About Me

Hello, My name is Dorian for all of you who didn't see the big name wrapped in teal. I was born in San Jose on January 15th 1992. And have been raised in Milpitas for pretty much my whole life. Nothing really interesting ever really happens here, so growing up I used to spend alot of time using public transit to get around and adventure the Bay Area. I like being active (when movitated) I'll hike the many hill throughout the Bay or drive thru them. A real passion of mine is rock climbing, it's a great way to workout and get me centered. When I'm not being active i spend most of my time playing video games such as Overwatch and PlayersUnkown's Battleground, looking for new music or watching anime.
